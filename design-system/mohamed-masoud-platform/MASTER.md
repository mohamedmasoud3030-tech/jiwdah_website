# LENA Digital House — Design System

## Global direction

- Pattern: immersive digital-house experience with modular Bento grids.
- Mode support: full light mode and full dark mode.
- Conversion strategy: cinematic opening, clear solution tracks, honest LENA Lab concept labeling, project exploration, and direct contact CTA.
- Section order: opening scene, connected solutions, LENA Lab experiences, process, and contact.

## Visual language

- Deep navy surfaces with blue, violet, cyan, and restrained warm highlights.
- Glass navigation, thin borders, soft glow, aurora layers, colored smoke, and subtle motion.
- Arabic headings: `Noto Kufi Arabic`.
- Arabic body: `IBM Plex Sans Arabic`.
- English: `Inter`.

## Required accessibility and UX rules

- Lucide SVG icons only.
- Minimum touch target: `44px`.
- Maintain readable contrast.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- No horizontal mobile scrolling.
- Concept projects must be labeled clearly as LENA Lab concepts.
- Do not fabricate testimonials, team members, or verified business results.
